# Registration Fees

The participation fee for the summer school are, as follows:

#### Standard Participants
- **Early Bird Registration (before 30th June 2026):** €350  
- **Standard Registration (after 1 August  2026):** €420  

#### Participants with I-RIM Membership
- **Early Bird Registration (before 30th June 2026):** €250  
- **Standard Registration (after 1st August  2026):** €320

The registration fee covers:

- Lectures  
- Lunch  
- Access to robots  
- Welcome drinks  
- Social dinner
